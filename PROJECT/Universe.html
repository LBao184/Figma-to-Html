UNIVERSE

The universe includes all existing matter, energy, and space.
It is believed to have begun with the Big Bang about 13.8 billion years ago.
Since then, the universe has been continuously expanding.
It contains planets, stars, galaxies, and subatomic particles.
The observable universe is about 93 billion light-years in diameter.
Its true size remains unknown and may be nearly infinite.
